# SEO Best Practices

## On-Page SEO

- Title tags: 50-60 characters
- Meta descriptions: 150-160 characters
- One H1 per page
- Hierarchical headings (H2, H3, H4)
- Internal linking strategy

## Technical SEO

- XML Sitemap
- Robots.txt
- Structured Data
- Mobile-Friendly Design
- Fast Load Times

## Off-Page SEO

- Quality backlinks
- Social signals
- Local SEO optimization
